Chie - Hairmaster

Chie Sugawara has earned a faithful following in New York City, after residencies at leading hair salons Aveda and Laicale in the Soho neighborhood of New York City for over 15 years. She has also acted as consultant to Aveda and Avon for the hair and beauty markets in the US and Japan, as well as providing hair and makeup styling for numerous fashion shows. Salon Orchard is Chie's first boutique hair salon, located in the trendy Lower East Side. She specializes in cutting edge coloring techniques, highlights, painting, balayage, vibrant color, modern cuts, razor cutting, blowouts, as well as special occasion hair. She is also trained in hair straightening treatments. Chie loves her job, and her enthusiasm for her work shows through her dedication to create her client’s perfect style.
